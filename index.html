Plato organizes your knowledge and your life.

The internet has brought an unprecedented wealth of knowledge. Not only is the amount of new content overwhelming, all of that new content is more interactive, dynamic, and personal than ever before. However, even though the internet is now almost half a century old, we still use old tools to interact with it. Even "high tech" cloud software uses the same static "notebooks" to save isolated snippets from our favorite parts of the web. These old school analogs were useful in understanding the digital realm when we first set foot in it, but now is the time for tools that were made from the ground up for the internet.

Plato is a new suite of productivity apps being designed to take control of your digital life. Orbit is an aggregator for your entire online life, bringing together social media, email, messaging, and news into one data stream. Almanac is notetaking made for the modern age. Ground Control is a todo and calendar app that can quickly and intelligently answer the simple but difficult question of "what's next?"
